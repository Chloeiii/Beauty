All About Etude House

The most popular cosmetic brand among South Korean high school girls.

In 1985, Etude House was found by a company called Amore Pacific. It opened its first retail store in Seoul in 2005. At that time ,Etude House is the first company that pay attention to " Color-Make-Up".

The meaning of the name " etude" is just a French translate " study and practice". Not only the pink outlay but also the price attract many Asian high school girls. And its make up play culture which is " let customers to see their own beauty and inner princess while enjoying and playing with make-up" make its company and products different from the other traditional cosmetic companies. Although Etude House opened its first retail store later than the other brands , it expand its market quickly. After two years since its first store in Seoul, its first oversea store opened in Thailand. Right now, Etude House is popular among all Asian countries and it isnot hard for people to find a Etude House retail store in main countries all over the Asia.

The success of Etude house is not only because its unique ideas about make up but also the selection of Spokespersons and models.
