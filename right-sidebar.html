3CE

The well-known clothing brand Stylenanda initiated 3CE in january 2009, most products of which are pink, orange, and beige.

3CE (3 Concept Eyes) is now one of the most popular cosmetics brand in Korea, although the history of this brand is not quite long, which is only 5 years. The products of 3CE is easy to use and of good effects, so that they are welcome towards Koran stardom, especially when a soap opera named "My Love From The Star" was broadcasted in recent years, the actress uesd 3CE quite often, that's why it becomes so popular within only 5 years.

The BB cream, eye liner, eye shadow, pore silky balm, lip color, nail lacquer, and all kinds of bags and cloth are very popular and fit for young ladies. The concept of 3CE is making ladies young and dynamic.

Also, the price of 3CE is really economical, whick makes this brand well-accepted all around the world.
